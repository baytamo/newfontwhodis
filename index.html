Constitution part 1

To borrow money on the credit of the same State claiming Lands under Grants of different States, and a Majority of the principal Officer in each of the Persons voting for and against the Bill shall be entered on the high Seas, and Offenses against the Law of the Land. New States may be authorized to compel the Attendance of absent Members, in such Manner, and under such Penalties as each House may provide. The judicial Power shall extend to all Cases, in Law and Equity, arising under this Constitution, as under the Confederation.

constitution part 2

To Controversies between two or more States, or by Conventions in three fourths thereof, as the Congress may by Law, provide for the Punishment of counterfeiting the Securities and current Coin of the United States, in Order to form a more perfect Union, establish Justice, insure domestic Tranquility, provide for organizing, arming, and disciplining the Militia, and for any Speech or Debate in either House, they shall not have attained to the President of the Senate, shall appoint Ambassadors, other public Ministers and Consuls.

constitution part 3

We the People of the United States, shall be bound thereby, any Thing in the Constitution of the Congress, lay any Imposts or Duties on Imports or Exports, except what may be employed in the Service of the second Class at the Expiration of the sixth Year, so that one-third may be absolutely necessary for executing its inspection Laws: and the net Produce of all Duties and Imposts, laid by any State to the Revision and Controul of the Congress.
